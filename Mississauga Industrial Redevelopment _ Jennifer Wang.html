ABOUT THE PROPERTY 项目介绍

Future-Focused Facility

面向未来的现代化厂房

This new industrial redevelopment offers businesses an exceptional opportunity to secure space in one of Mississauga's most established and highly accessible employment corridors.

这栋全新工业重建项目地处密西沙加最成熟、交通最便利的商业走廊之一，为企业提供了不可多得的优质空间选择。

Designed with modern functionality and sustainability in mind, the building features Net Zero Ready specifications, supporting occupiers seeking efficient, future-focused industrial space.

项目在设计上充分兼顾现代功能性与可持续发展理念，达到净零能耗（Net Zero Ready）标准，适合追求高效、面向未来的工业用户。

Strategically positioned just south of Highway 401 and Mississauga Road, the property offers excellent connectivity across the GTA, convenient transit access, and a deep, diverse regional labour pool.

项目紧邻401高速公路与Mississauga Road，交通网络四通八达，公交接驳便利，同时可就近获得大多伦多地区丰富多元的劳动力资源。
